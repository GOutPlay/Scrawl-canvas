May 2020 results

Currently experiencing a number of issues and bugs in the mix between phrase and styles code:

  • For single-line text, the initial letter's gradient appears to be mis-positioned, compared to the other letters in the Phrase. Can also occur randomly (and occasionally) for individual characters in multi-line text
  • Phrases locked to a path - all letters appear to use the initial letter's take on the gradient it should be displaying.
  • Flipped Phrases have issues.
  • Issues with multi-line text that has been scaled.
